TRANSMISSION & General Auto Repair

For bringing your vehicle into our Eagle Transmission Center, we will offer each of our customers:

FREE Towing w/ Repair, FREE Computer Diagnostics, and a FREE 28pt Inspection


Transmission Repair
This service is for the computer diagnostics, removal, and repair of your transmission. Most locations offer a standard warranty of 3yr / 36,000 miles *.

Differential Gears Service
This service consist of a diagnostic inspection, removal, and repair of the damaged gears, as well as replacing the fluid.

Driveshaft Repair
Whether your vehicle needs a driveshaft balance, shortened, new u-joints... Send it in! We will repair & install universal joints and the driveshaft.

Transfer Case Repair
A transfer case is part of your 4 wheel drive system and is found in your four wheel drive and all wheel drive vehicles. This service provides an inspection, removal, and repair of the gears, bearings, gaskets, and seals in the transfer case.

Transmission Fluid Replacement
Nearly nine out of ten transmission failures are due to overheating and fluid contamination. Just like the oil in your engine, automatic transmission fluid (ATF) suffers from heat, friction, and electrochemical degradation. This service provides a replacement of the fluid filter and gasket.

Clutch Replacement
This service will replace the clutch, pressure plate, and throw out bearing. We will also resurface the flywheel.
